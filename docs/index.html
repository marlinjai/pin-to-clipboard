Pin to Clipboard

One-click copy of any Pinterest pin's image, straight from the board.

Pinterest only lets you "Copy Image" from a pin's detail page. Pin to Clipboard adds a small red copy button to every pin on the board, home feed, and search results : one click and the image is on your clipboard, ready to paste anywhere.

Open source, no analytics, no telemetry, no remote code. The extension only runs on pinterest.com.
