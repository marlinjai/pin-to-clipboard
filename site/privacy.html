Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-23.

What this extension is

Pin to Clipboard is a Chrome extension that adds a one-click copy button to Pinterest pins. It only runs on pinterest.com.

What we collect

Nothing. No analytics, no telemetry, no remote code, no third-party services.

What's stored

Two user preferences (image quality and video action) are saved via Chrome's storage.sync. That is the only state the extension keeps. There is no account, no identifier, no log.

What we access

When you click the copy button, the extension fetches the specific media you clicked from Pinterest's CDN (i.pinimg.com for images, v.pinimg.com for videos). No other network requests are made. The fetched bytes are written to your clipboard locally and immediately discarded.

Sharing or selling data

Never. There is no data to share.
